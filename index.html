How to Win — Full Guide
🎯 Scoring
Each round: Base Stat + Move Bonus + 🎲 Luck (1–6) = Total.
The higher total wins the round and deals damage.
⚔️ Move Types
Power attacks give big bonus on their matching challenge type (e.g. Neural Strike → +3 on Coding).
Shield moves absorb incoming damage instead of boosting your score.
Super moves cost 100% energy but are devastatingly powerful.
🧠 When to Use Which Move
• If the challenge matches your fighter's strength → use the power attack for max bonus.
• If it's a bad matchup or your opponent is strong → use a shield move to survive.
• If your energy meter is full → unleash the super move for a game-changer!
💥 Advanced Mechanics
Critical Hits — 15% chance to deal double damage.
Combo Streaks — Win multiple rounds in a row for +1 bonus damage per streak.
Energy Meter — Builds +20 (winner), +30 (loser), +10 (tie) each round.
Piercing — Some supers ignore the opponent's shield entirely.
🏆 How to Win the Match
First player to reduce the opponent's HP to 0 wins. If all 7 rounds pass, the player with more HP remaining wins.
